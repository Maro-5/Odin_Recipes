Korean Chicken Fried Rice

Click here for a video guide by Panacea Palm

Calories & Macros

Per meal: 545 calories

53g P | 48g C | 15g F

Makes 5 servings

Ingredients

  • 800g boneless chicken thighs
  • 3 eggs
  • 200g 0% Greek yogurt
  • 60g light mayo
  • 250g dry white rice
  • 150g frozen veg
  • Green onions
  • Half a lemon
  • 105g gochujang paste
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • White or black pepper
  • Ginger powder
  • Salt
  • 20g brown sugar or brown sugar alternative
  • 30ml soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1-2 tsp toasted sesame oil
  • 15ml rice vinegar
  • 2 tsp honey
  • cooking spray

Directions

Step 1 Chicken Thighs

Add the following to a bowl:

  • 800g boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • 2 tsp garlic powder tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp white pepper (or black pepper)
  • 1 tsp ginger
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 75g gochujang paste
  • 20g brown sugar (or brown sugar alternative)
  • cooking oil (or spray)

Mix well then place in a baking tray on some baking paper and:

Oven Bake 200°C for 25-30 minutes

Airfry 180°C for 15-20 minutes

(Flip at halfway)

Dice the chicken after it's finished cooking and put to the side

Step 2 Creamy Gochujang Sauce

Add the following to a blender:

  • 200g 0% Greek yoghurt
  • 60g light mayo
  • 30g gochujang
  • 15ml rice vinegar
  • 2 tsp honey
  • juice from half a lemon
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • salt and pepper to taste

Blend it up till smooth and store in a bottle

Step 3 Fried Rice

  1. Add 150g frozen mixed veg and 3 chopped green onions to a pan and cook till soft
  2. Add 3 beaten eggs and scramble till fluffy
  3. Add 750g cooked cold white rice (250g dry, cook beforehand and refrigerate), 30ml soy sauce, 1 tbsp oyster sauce, add your diced chicken then drizzle with some sesame oil and mix well
  4. Plate and Enjoy

Storage & Heating

Store for up to 5 days in the fridge

To reheat, add the contents to a microwave safe bowl, add a tsp of water and microwave covered with a damp paper towel for 3-4 minutes till heated through, drizzle with the creamy gochujang sauce and enjoy!
