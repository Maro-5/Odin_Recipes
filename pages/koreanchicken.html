Korean Chicken Fried Rice

Click here for a video guide by Panacea Palm

Description

If youre trying to lose fat and gain muscle this high protein korean chicken fried rice is what you'll need.
Its easy to make, tastes better than the restaurant and stores well so you can easily prep a whole weeks worth.

Calories & Macros

Per meal: 545 calories

53g P | 48g C | 15g F

Makes 5 servings

Ingredients

  • 800g boneless chicken thighs
  • 3 eggs
  • 200g 0% Greek yogurt
  • 60g light mayo
  • 250g dry white rice
  • 150g frozen veg
  • Green onions
  • Half a lemon
  • 105g gochujang paste
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • White or black pepper
  • Ginger powder
  • Salt
  • 20g brown sugar or brown sugar alternative
  • 30ml soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1-2 tsp toasted sesame oil
  • 15ml rice vinegar
  • 2 tsp honey
  • cooking spray

Directions

Chicken Thighs

  1. Add the following to a bowl:
    • 800g boneless skinless chicken thighs
    • 2 tsp garlic powder tsp onion powder
    • 1 tsp white pepper (or black pepper)
    • 1 tsp ginger
    • 2 tsp salt
    • 75g gochujang paste
    • 20g brown sugar (or brown sugar alternative)
    • cooking oil (or spray)
  2. Mix well then place in a baking tray on some baking paper and:

    3. Oven Bake 200°C for 25-30 minutes

    Airfry 180°C for 15-20 minutes

  3. (Flip at halfway)
  4. Dice the chicken after it's finished cooking and put to the side

Creamy Gochujang Sauce

  1. Add the following to a blender:
    • 200g 0% Greek yoghurt
    • 60g light mayo
    • 30g gochujang
    • 15ml rice vinegar
    • 2 tsp honey
    • juice from half a lemon
    • 1 tsp garlic powder
    • salt and pepper to taste
  2. Blend it up till smooth and store in a bottle

Fried Rice

  1. Add 150g frozen mixed veg and 3 chopped green onions to a pan and cook till soft
  2. Add 3 beaten eggs and scramble till fluffy
  3. Add 750g cooked cold white rice (250g dry, cook beforehand and refrigerate), 30ml soy sauce, 1 tbsp oyster sauce, add your diced chicken then drizzle with some sesame oil and mix well
  4. Plate and Enjoy
